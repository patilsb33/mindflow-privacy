MindFlow – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

MindFlow is designed to help you understand and improve your digital wellbeing. We take your privacy seriously and keep things simple: your data stays on your device.

1. Data we collect

MindFlow does not collect, upload, or share any personal data to our servers or to any third parties. All information used by the app is stored locally on your device.

Examples of data stored locally may include:

  • Your mood check-ins and notes
  • Screen time and usage statistics provided by the operating system
  • Streaks, goals, achievements, and wellbeing scores
  • Settings such as language, limits, and notification preferences

2. No account, no cloud, no tracking

  • No account required: You can use MindFlow without creating an account.
  • No cloud sync: We do not sync your data to any cloud service.
  • No analytics tracking: We do not use third‑party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No ads: We do not show personalized ads based on your data.

3. Permissions

MindFlow may request certain system permissions (for example, usage access or notification access) so it can:

  • Read app usage time to calculate your screen time and focus metrics
  • Show wellbeing reminders and notifications (e.g., eye strain, posture, bedtime)

These permissions are used only on your device to provide app features. The data accessed via these permissions is not sent to any server.

4. How your data is stored

All data is stored locally in the app’s private storage on your device. It is managed by the operating system and is not accessible to other apps (unless your device is rooted or compromised).

If you uninstall the app or clear its data, all stored information will be permanently deleted from your device.

5. Children’s privacy

MindFlow is a general wellbeing app and does not knowingly collect any personal data from children. Since we do not run servers or accounts, we do not have any way to identify or track individual users.

6. Third‑party services

MindFlow does not integrate third‑party analytics, advertising networks, or external data processors that receive your personal data from the app.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect improvements or new features. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
